About

Edible is a website that we created to introduce varieties of Malaysian cuisine to people. The first thing you should know about Malaysians is they are passionate about food. So, this website helps people to differentiate Malaysians food that are categorised based on races in Malaysia which are Malay, Chinese and Indian. Each race has their own unique traditional food.

We also include the best place to get the food so it may help the tourists or even our local people to try them. You may look for the best location to try some dishes, so here, we provide the best place for you to try the dishes with some reviews from those wo tried them!
