About Us

Edible is a website that we created to introduce the varieties of Malaysian cuisine to people. The first thing you should know about Malaysians is they are passionate about food. Malaysia, situated in South East Asia, has been home to many different cultures. Given its location, trade and colonial history, it has been influenced greatly by different parts of the world. It has very diverse population consisting of many different ethnic groups, with over half of the population being Malay, one quarter Chinese, and about 7% Indian. So, this website helps people to differentiate Malaysians food that are categorised based on races in Malaysia which are Malay, Chinese and Indian. Each race has their own unique traditional food.

You might look for the best restaurant to try some dishes and now you are in the right page! So here we are providing some suggestions about the best place to get the food so it may help the tourists or even our local people to try them out. We also include some reviews from the people who have tried the dishes and provide the map direction that ease you to go to that place. You also can share your own review in our website after trying the dishes!
